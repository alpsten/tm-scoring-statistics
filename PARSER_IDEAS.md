# Log Parser — Future Ideas

Features to circle back to once cards played + milestones are done.

## Tracked in DB when ready

- **Turn order per generation** — who went first in each gen (rotates); link to win rate
- **Standard projects used** — who uses Greenery/City/Ocean/etc. and how often; correlates with engine quality
- **Awards funded** — who funded which award (already in parser, table TBD)
- **First player** — overall and per-generation; store in game_sessions or separate table
- **Parameter contributions per generation** — who raised which parameter and when (currently only totals)
- **Colony trades** — who traded with which colony each generation
- **Draft picks** — if playing with draft variant, which cards were passed vs. kept

## Analysis ideas (once data exists)

- **Cards by generation** — which generation each card was played; does playing high-VP cards early correlate with winning?
- **Engine speed** — when does a player start generating significant MC production? proxy for engine quality
- **Card synergy** — which card pairs appear together most often among winners
- **Comeback tracking** — who was behind after gen 4 but won anyway
- **Milestone race** — how many players were close to each milestone when it was claimed
